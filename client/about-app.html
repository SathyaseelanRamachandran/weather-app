What can you do with this web application?

1. Look up the weather status of a city

With this app you can search for the weather of more than 200000 cities in the world

2. Look up the weather forecast of a city

With this app you can search for the weather forecast of the next five days

3. Save the current weather status to your weather history

With this app you can search for the weather status of a city and save it for later with your name and password(keep your password save)

4. Display your weather history

With this app you can display all your saved weather history (of course if you have your password)

5. Change your name in the system

With this app you can change your name in your weather history (of course if you have your password)

6. Delete your weather history if you want to

With this app you can delete your weather history from the system (of course if you have your password)
